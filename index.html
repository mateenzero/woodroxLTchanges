Problems in Urban living

The world is rapidly urbanising with significant changes in our living standards, lifestyles, social behaviour and health.

Health challenges particularly evident in cities relate to water, environment, violence, noncommunicable diseases (cardiovascular diseases, cancers, diabetes and chronic respiratory diseases), unhealthy diets.

Why Zaheerabad?

City is neighboring to Maharashtra & Karnataka borders, trading revenue is good.

Mahindra’s tractor manufacturing plant, other industries include sugar refining, rubber and chemical manufacturing units that has provided great employment.

City is turning industrial hub with various projects lined up along with recent setups. NIMZ is going to change the shape of Zahirabad.

Connectivity

Its around 100KM from Hyderabad, 4-Way ExpressWay, 2hrs Journey by a normal car, ORR Exit from Patancheru.

RTC buses connecting to various places of the states of Telangana, AP, Maharashtra and Karnataka.

Trains running to Bangalore, Nanded, Shirdi and Pune

Developments around

Mahindra & Mahindra manufacturing plant is around 5KM away.

NIMZs to come up in 12,500 acres, to generate a million jobs when operational.

Great amount of land is already absorbed for the same and being developed which is located close to Legend’s Township.

Legalities & Technicalities

Legend’s township is a DTCP (Directorate of Town & Country Planning) layout fulfilling all the recommendations from DTCP.

All the property documents have records for more than 40years and are verified by legal experts thus one can directly go for bank loans moreover the project is pre-approved by several banks.

Sports & Entertainment means

Loads of open green space, Avenue plantation with Parks, Children playing area, Jogging / Cycling tracks and for sports lovers we have separate ground/pitches for Cricket, Volleyball, Football & Lawn Tennis.

Open air theatre show movies in the evenings, stage shows can also be played.

Club house with party halls with scenic rooftop swimming pool on the second floor.

Safety & Security

Since its a gated community, entire area will have boundary wall in the surroundings 24hrs security with CCTV surveillance, and entry is restricted to only residents with electronic access card. Visitors will be given entry with residents approval.
